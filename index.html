rats: sleeping

it started with a single sleeping Norway rat. while the rat napped, the instructor picked up the phone and called every pest control company, research lab, and exotic pet store in the county.

the pitch was simple: "i have rats. you need rats. let's talk."

by the time the rat woke up, three deals were already signed. $6,000 in 72 hours. the rat never even knew.

the 3-step method:

step 1 — find a sleeping rat

Any rat will do. The key is that they're asleep and cannot object to your business decisions on their behalf. Norway rats sleep 12+ hours a day. Ideal business partners.

step 2 — cold call everyone

Research labs, pet stores, film productions (rats are very cinematic), haunted houses, and eccentric millionaires. Cast a wide net. Someone always needs a rat.

step 3 — close before they wake up

"This particular rat won't be available forever" is technically true about all living things. Close the deal. Collect payment. Let the rat keep sleeping.
